Services

Cooks

Personalized Meal Preparation Our skilled cooks provide personalized meal preparation, tailored to the dietary needs and preferences of each client. Whether it's for seniors, individuals with specific health conditions, or busy families, we ensure that every meal is nutritious, delicious, and made with care.

House maids

Comprehensive Household Support Our trained house maids offer a wide range of household services, including cleaning, laundry, and organizing, to keep your home tidy, comfortable, and well-maintained. We take care of daily chores so you can focus on what matters most.

Patient care

Comprehensive Patient Care We offer personalized patient care services, tailored to meet the unique medical and personal needs of individuals recovering from illness, injury, or surgery, as well as those with chronic conditions. Our trained caregivers ensure comfort, safety, and emotional support.

Baby care

Professional Infant and Child Care Our dedicated caregivers are trained to provide professional baby care, ensuring the safety, comfort, and well-being of your infant. From newborns to toddlers, we offer attentive care to meet the unique needs of every child.

Nurses for bedriddens

Skilled Nursing for Bedridden Patients Our trained and certified nurses provide specialized care for bedridden patients, ensuring their comfort, safety, and well-being. We offer both medical and personal care to enhance the quality of life for patients confined to bed.

Real estates

Comprehensive Real Estate Solutions At The Service People, we offer a full range of real estate services tailored to meet the needs of homeowners, investors, and renters. Whether you’re buying, selling, or leasing, our experienced team is here to guide you through every step of the process.

Security guards

Trained and Licensed Professionals All our security guards are highly trained and licensed professionals with extensive experience in various security environments. They are equipped with the skills necessary to handle a range of security challenges effectively

Physiotheraphy

Experienced and Licensed Physiotherapists Our team consists of licensed and experienced physiotherapists who specialize in various areas, including orthopedics, neurology, geriatrics, and sports rehabilitation. They utilize evidence-based practices to ensure effective treatment

Matrimonial services

Personalized Matchmaking Our matrimonial services offer personalized matchmaking to help individuals find their ideal partners. We take the time to understand each clients preferences, values, and goals to ensure meaningful connections.

Acting drivers

Professional and Experienced Drivers Our acting drivers are highly trained and experienced professionals committed to providing reliable transportation services. They are adept at navigating various routes and ensuring timely arrivals.

Welcome girls

Event Assistance and Coordination Our welcome girls assist in event coordination, greeting guests, managing guest lists, and directing attendees to their designated areas. Their organizational skills ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Hotel staffs and needs

Comprehensive Staffing Solutions We provide comprehensive staffing solutions for hotels, ensuring that you have the right personnel to meet your operational needs. From front desk staff to housekeeping, we cover all aspects of hotel staffing.

Services boys&girls for events

Professional Event Staff Our service boys and girls are trained professionals dedicated to providing excellent support during events. They ensure that every detail is attended to, creating a seamless experience for both hosts and guests.

Packers & movers

Comprehensive Relocation Solutions Our packers and movers services offer comprehensive relocation solutions for residential and commercial clients, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free moving experience.

Agri farmers

Skilled Agricultural Workforce We provide skilled and experienced agricultural workers to support various farming operations, from planting and harvesting to maintenance and equipment operation.
